\section{Reports}
\label{sec:reports}

Each section is attached to reports, if requested in configuration (see options \texttt{cnfInfo}, \texttt{tgtInfo}, \texttt{sysInfo} and \texttt{netInfo} in \ref{sec:configuration}).
